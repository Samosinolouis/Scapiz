Our Mission

Our mission is to revolutionize the accessibility and dissemination of academic research at Rizal Technological University - Senior High School (RTU-SHS) by developing SCAPIZ, a digital library platform. We aim to provide students and researchers with a seamless, user-friendly interface for accessing, publishing, and engaging with local studies, thereby fostering an efficient and supportive academic environment.
